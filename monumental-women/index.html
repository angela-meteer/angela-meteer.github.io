#MonumentalWomen

2016—Social Media—Branding

The Elizabeth Cady Stanton & Susan B. Anthony Statue Fund (yeah, it’s a mouthful) is a nonprofit, all-volunteer organization dedicated to placing the first statue of a historical woman in New York City’s Central Park. As an intern at Thinkso Creative, I was responsible for creating all Instagram content and brand collateral for the fund’s Monumental Women campaign—part of a larger rebrand for the organization. The brand is focused around vibrant colors, clean typography, and succinct messaging to capture the attention of passerby.
