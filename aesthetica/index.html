Aesthetica Magazine

2017—Publication

Aesthetica is a British art and culture magazine that covers visual art, photography, music, fashion, and architecture. In this conceptual redesign, the masthead is encased in a square where the letters change position from issue to issue, like the reorginazation of walls in a gallery space.
